0. Introduction

0.0 What is this?

This is an opinionated guide to building native mobile apps with NativeScript. You by no means have to follow the advice this guide prescribes, however, the very opinionated authors of this guide feel that following these guidelines will help you produce high-quality, maintenance friendly, and performant apps with NativeScript.

Feel free to ignore tips that don't apply to your projects, or to build upon this advice to meet your needs. If you find an error in this guide, or would like to suggest new guidelines, feel free to open an issue in this guide's GitHub repo.
